Conference Awards

IEEE-NEMS 2027 will present the following awards to recognize outstanding contributions:

CM Ho Best Paper in Micro/Nanofluidics

Certificate and cash prize for the best paper in the field of micro/nanofluidics. Sponsored in honor of Prof. Chih-Ming Ho.

Best Conference Paper Award

Certificate and cash prize for the best overall paper presented at the conference.

Best Student Paper Award

Certificate and cash prize for the best paper whose first author is a student. The student must present the paper in person.

Best Poster Award

Certificate and cash prize for the best poster presentation.

Selection Process

  • The Technical Program Committee (TPC) will nominate up to 5 finalists for each award category.
  • The Awards Committee will evaluate finalists based on both the written paper and the oral/poster presentation.
  • Awards will be announced during the conference banquet.
