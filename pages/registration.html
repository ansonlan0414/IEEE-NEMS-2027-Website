Registration Policy

  • Each accepted paper requires at least one full (non-student) registration.
  • Each full registration covers up to one paper. Additional papers require extra paper fees.
  • Registration includes access to all technical sessions, conference materials, coffee breaks, and the conference banquet.

Cancellation Policy

A 50% refund will be issued for cancellations received before the early bird registration deadline. No refunds will be given after that date.
